07 — rules of use

Principles, Not Prescriptions

ALWAYS Use monospace for numbers, labels, tags, and the × mark
ALWAYS Include the stripe somewhere — header, section break, or footer
ALWAYS Use the gold breath line between major sections
PREFER Dark backgrounds for openings and closings (the noir sandwich)
PREFER Left-aligned body text, centered only for titles and marks
NEVER Use monospace for body paragraphs — it loses its signal value
NEVER Use the stripe colors without their meaning — green is always growth, red is always rigor
